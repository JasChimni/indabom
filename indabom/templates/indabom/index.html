    Indabom is a simple bill of materials management tool built by hardware engineers.

    thumb_up

    Simple

    Many Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) and Bill of Materials (BOM) tools have way more features than most people actually use, making the tool complicated especially for hobbiests, startups, and freelancers. We keep Indabom simple, focusing on your bill of materials, component sourcing, and revision control.

    cloud_done

    Integrated

    Integreated with modern tools like Octopart to pull in the latest sourcing information and optimizing your BOM for cost, and Google Drive for quick access to your files (coming soon).

    settings

    Open Source

    Indabom was started because excel wasn't enough, and PLM tools were too complex. It's open source utilizing the Django web framework written in Python so you can participate in the development and contribute to the features and concepts.

    Indabom auto-generates internal part numbers with a simple defined numbering scheme.

    Don't worry about coming up with a complicated numbering scheme. We have a pre-defined component numbering scheme including a part category prefix, the item number, and a two-digit number variation. Once a part is created, use the BOM uploading tool to batch upload an assembly via csv.

    Not what you want? This bill of materials management tool is open source so you can customize to your pleasing.
